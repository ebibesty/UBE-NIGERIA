ABOUT ICT SCHOOLS

Seidougha Best is my name. I obtained a B.Sc degree in Computer Science and Informatics from Federal University, Otuoke [FUO], Bayelsa State, Nigeria. I'm a Software Engineer, Web Application Developer, Web Designer and a teacher by profession. My blog ictschools.blogspot.com carters for Learners in all categories, ranging from primary education level to tertiary education level and also those who are interested in vocational skills In Computer science and ICT soft skills. Ictschools Blog is a both user and kid-friendly online ict blog set up to inform and educate children, adolescents and adults (parents) on current trends in Information and Communication Technology and Computing Education thereby promoting Computing awareness among people (kids) in Nigeria in particular and Africa at large. At Ictschools, we prepare children for the future(Information Age) which is now here with us by training them in fundamental/basic computer skills which will provide the needed competitive advantage for them to become global solution providers technologically. We do this by providing computer training (services) to children within the ages of 5 and 17 in camps, schools, churches and any gathering of children where we are invited. We also offer P2P (Person-to-Person) tutoring in the form of private home lessons.

Features of our programs include:
Computational Thinking / Algorithm | Flowchart
Desktop Publishing | Web Development
Programming: Qbasic | Python | Javascript | Android
